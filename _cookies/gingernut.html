--- layout : cookie title: ginger nut image_path: https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/d/d1/AfghanBiscuit.jpg --- An ginger nut biscuit is a traditional New Zealand biscuit made from flour, butter, cornflakes, sugar and cocoa powder, topped with chocolate icing and a half walnut. The recipe[1] has a high proportion of butter, and relatively low sugar, and no leavening (rising agent), giving it a soft, dense and rich texture, with crunchiness from the cornflakes, rather than from a high sugar content.
